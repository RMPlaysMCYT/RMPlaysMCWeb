Simple Vehicles Addon: Karts

The Simple Vehicles Addon - Karts is a minecraft addon that offers five unique karts with stylish designs and smooth functionality. These karts are craftable in survival mode, allowing players to integrate them seamlessly into their gameplay without needing creative mode. They are also fully respawnable, ensuring a consistent and enjoyable experience. This add-on is perfect for players who love fast-paced action and unique transportation options.

    Features:

  • Craftable Karts
    Respawanable Karts
    Adds Custom Cinematic
