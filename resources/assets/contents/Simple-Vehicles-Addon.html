Simple Vehicles Addon

This add-on adds up to 120 vehicles in Minecraft and this is my biggest add-on that I ever made it for you and to everyone who love vehicles. It could be suitable to City and Role-Play maps.

This addon was created in 2019 as I was watching vehicle mods when I was just in high school. Days back then, I created my own version in 2018, which is actually the beginning of the Simple Vehicles Addon, but I made my mistake, and I'll never lose my interest in making addons like this until now, and here we are. This has over 100 vehicles in your Minecraft world, and there are lots of things you would like to enjoy on any platform, like mobile and PC.
